PhenoMeNal is funded by the European Commission's Horizon2020 programme, grant agreement number 654241. It is driven by a consortium of 14 European research groups with strong experience in tool and methods development for large data acquisition, integration and analysis for metabolic phenotypes, genome and cross-omics data.
